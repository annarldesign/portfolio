Innovation & Product Development - 5E3

Used at the beginning of an online showcase for an Innovation and Product development module at Trinity College Dublin. The video would make more sense with a voice over, however it was to be played as people “arrived” at the online event, and for this reason the voice over was left out as people would be joining at different times and could be confused if arriving in the "middle" of the video.

What I learned

  • Improved my adobe illustration skills when drawing elements for the animated videos.
  • The innovation and product development video was made in May during lockdown. No footage could be filmed so all of the footage had to be sourced from previous videos. It was challenging to develop a semi coherent video with random bits of footage. But this was a nice challenge.
  • Improved my interpretation of clients ideas - Sometimes it is difficult to imagine exactly what other people are imagining! I found it best to roughly design the video scenes and show the client this for feedback, instead of spending hours perfecting something, for it then to be changed, wasting a lot of time.
