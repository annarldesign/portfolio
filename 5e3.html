Robotics & Innovation Lab

Research Engineer & Teaching Fellow at Trinity college dublin's robotics and innovation lab

Overview of Work

After finishing my studies in Trinity I was offered a position as a research engineer in the Robotics and Innovation Lab. Here I taught design, innovation, product development and computing modules, while managing budgets and carrying out research as part of the engineering team responsible for assisting businesses availing of Enterprise Ireland's Innovation Voucher Scheme.

Design Thinking Mentor

Guided 6 international teams through the design process for three commercially driven solutions across manufacturing, health and education sectors.

Worked as part of the global SUGAR design network, which brings students and companies together to solve real world problems over a 9 month design cycle.

Mentored these teams in all areas of the design process covering needfinding, benchmarking, concept design & development, patent research, user research & testing and iterative prototyping based on user feedback. One of these teams (Volymi) went on to receive funding and is currently working on developing their product commercially.

Teaching Fellow

Taught master and undergraduate students the following modules:

  • MATLAB - Introduction to Computing
  • User Centered Design - User research, interview techniques, personas, stakeholder & empathy maps, needfinding & benchmarking, concept development
  • Innovation in Product Development - User testing, iterative design, rapid prototyping, human factor design, team & design workshops, user insights, presentation & pitching skills

Research

Diverse research carried out in many areas including manufacturing, medical devices, smart bins, online education delivery and keeping the elderly connected with their loved ones online.

What I learned

  • Improved my design skills while designing visuals, wireframes, videos and documents, using Adobe Creative Suite, for successful applications to Enterprise Ireland for funding.
  • Improved project management while working on multiple projects simultaneously.
  • Gained experience working on global remote teams, even before covid!
