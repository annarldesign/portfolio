Google Maps Parking

Need

When I'm going to a new place, say for an appointment, in an area I don't really know, I often find it difficult to find the best place to park.

Solution

A button to click to find the nearest parking options to your location.

Apple Alarm

Issue

Currently (iosXXX) to change an alarm time you need to click edit and then the alarm. This means clicking twice, in different areas of the screen (top left and the alarm you want to edit). To add a new alarm you just have to click once, making it much easier to set an alarm quickly.

Solution

As someone who sets many alars for reminders etc. I always just add a new alarm as it is much quicer than editing an existing alarm. I'd like the option to be able to click into an existing alarm and edit the alarm time with just one click.

Apple Calculator

Issue

Not being able to see the calculation you just entered

Solution

Display the calculation!

Gmail iPhone App

Issue

Say you get an email to check in for a flight, and click the check in link within the email. You stay in the gmail app and fill in all your details and finally you need a booking reference or a code from your email to complete the check in. You have to go back out to the original email to get the code, sometimes resulting in loosing all of the data you originally filled in and inturn wasting time!

Solution

Copy code from email feature, similar to when you get a verification code text
