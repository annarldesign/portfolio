Pizza Oven

Built during lockdown!

Need

Something to keep me busy during lockdown & use up left over bricks. This project is not 100% complete yet, but it works and the pizza is tasty!

Steps Taken

After some research into DIY pizza ovens, rough hand drawings and CAD model drawn in Fusion360 were used to show my family what I was planning to do, once I got the OK, I got all the materials and with help from my boyfriend and family we began building the Pizza Oven.

  1. Created a mould for the pizza oven using an exercise ball & plywood
  2. Covered the mould in a mix of vermiculite and cement
  3. Let dry for about a month
  4. Dug out and lay concrete foundation
  5. Built a surround from left over bricks that were around the house
  6. Made a mould for concrete slab, filled with concrete & rebar and let dry
  7. Placed this onto of the base surround and made an enclosure for the heat sink with bricks
  8. Collected wine bottles and filled the heat sink with them and fire clay
  9. Placed fire bricks on top of this and then the pizza oven, secured with mortar
  10. Secured the chimney with fire cement

Another layer of vermiculite and concrete was put on the top of the oven dome as some smoke was escaping. Currently waiting for this, and a concrete counter top slab to dry.

Next Steps

  • Plaster and paint the oven dome
  • Place counter top on top of the support which has been built
  • Lay bricks at the front of the oven (aesthetic purposes only!)

What I learned

  • How to make cement & pizza dough!
